TOMATO DISEASE PREDICTION

Aim of this project is to help people to grow healthy tomatoes at their garden. Deep learning model has been trained to identify 9 different diseases and healthy tomatoes.

More

About the project

To train our model tomato leaf data has been used from Kaggle. All the images are resized to 64x64 for training and data augmentation techniques have been used to have robust estimates, like horizontal and vertical flipping, zooming, changing brightness.
